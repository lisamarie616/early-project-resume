Coding

Most of my career has been spent in accounting, which has never excited me. I recall one day a couple of years ago when I wondered what made me happiest in my work. I loved the Excel formulas--the more complicated the better. I loved writing them. I loved when they worked. I loved when they did something extremely useful. At that point, I had been curious about coding for awhile and began to dabble in things like Codeacademy and Udemy courses. I was instantly hooked. Coding was like writing Excel formulas, but better. There was more depth, more to learn, and even more useful things to create. I have continued my learning online and attended in person coding events. And, I have loved every minute of it.
